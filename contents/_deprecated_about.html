About me

Hi, I’m Lai Joon-Keat, you can just call me JK. I was born in Malaysia, and I leave for Taiwan since I was 19. In other words, about one third of my life (i.e. 11 years) was raised in Taiwan. Interestingly, my great grandparent was come from Guangzhou of China, so I’m native in Hakka and Cantonese.

I obtained Bachelor degree with double major from the department of plant industry and the department of biotechnology at National Pingtung University of Science and Technology. I'm currently studying for a doctoral degree in the department of plant industry. My expertise is on the field of plant monitoring and agronomic data analysis and modelling. I have been involved in several projects that are incorporating with Taiwan Agricultural Research Institute and Extension Station. For examples, I have been taking part of the establishment of phenotyping techniques that could be utilized in rice breeding and cultivation, a disease detection system for greenhouse-cultivated tomato, and et cetera>.
